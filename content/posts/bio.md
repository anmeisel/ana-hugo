Ana Meisel is a Polish-German web developer and videographer, born in Czechia and based in London, UK, where she produces online courses for the University of London, and occasionally teaches design at Goldsmiths, University of London. She builds websites as part of [4 Us & 4 Others](https://4us4others.com/?to=/) studio, runs [External Pages](https://externalpages.org/), an internet art gallery, and is one half of [Our Friend the Computer](https://www.ourfriendthe.computer/), a tech history podcast. Interests include luddism, maintainers culture, and the umwelt.
