Ana Meisel is a Polish-German web developer and technologist from Czechia, based in London, UK. She runs internet art gallery [External Pages](https://externalpages.org/), is one half of tech history podcast [Our Friend The Computer](https://www.ourfriendthe.computer/) and one third of research group [Superkilogirls](https://superkilogirls.com/). 

Interests include luddism and maintainer communities.
